### Description: 

The premise is that it's an app to connect college students based on their interests to group them together to do activities based on what hobbies they have in common. The app will put you in a group based on you most liked hobby where you can chat. It will recommend activities that have to do with that hobby in the general area in that specific group chat. This app is made to try to get to know people based on their interests without having to awkwardly strike conversations with them in real life at first (made for less sociable people), especially for freshman who are getting introduced to a completely brand new setting.

### Audience: 

Our app is catered towards college students (mainly freshman). The needs we addressed for college students are connecting, thriving, engaging, interaction with friends/new people, trying new things, moving, keeping busy, studying, exploring, having fun. We focused on connecting, interacting with new people, and keeping busy as the basis for our app's features/principles.

### How It All Works:

The layout of the app is pretty intuitive, with everything the user taps leading to the desired page. The user must save their username and password for future use. The user will go through the quiz at their own liking and present it with the answers that cater towards them the most.
